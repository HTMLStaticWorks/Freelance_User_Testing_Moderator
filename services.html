Research Methodologies

We combine academic rigor with agile product speed to deliver insights that actually move the needle on your KPIs.

Moderated Prototype Testing

Evaluate your design assumptions before engineering begins. We observe users navigating your Figma or Adobe XD prototypes and ask the "why" behind their actions.

  • Identify navigation friction points
  • Validate value proposition clarity
  • Test content hierarchy & information architecture

Discovery & Persona Interviews

Understand the problem space deeply. We conduct semi-structured interviews to uncover user mental models, current workarounds, and unmet needs.

  • Journey mapping sessions
  • Jobs-to-be-Done (JTBD) framework
  • Competitive sentiment analysis

Quantitative UX Benchmarking

Turn qualitative feedback into measurable metrics. We track standardized UX scores over time to prove the impact of design changes.

  • SUS & SEQ Scoring
  • Task Success Rate & Time-on-Task
  • Competitive benchmarking studies

Deep Research Synthesis

Data is useless without analysis. We rigorously code and tag session transcripts to identify recurring themes and statistically significant issues.

  • • Thematic analysis of qualitative data
  • • Severity rating of usability issues
  • • Affinity mapping of user sentiments

Executive Highlight Reels

Nothing builds empathy like watching a user struggle. We compile the most impactful clips into 2-5 minute montages.

  • • Annotated video clips
  • • Themed montages (e.g., "Navigation Friction")
  • • Sharable via secure dashboard links

Actionable Reporting

We don't just hand over raw data. You receive a presentation-ready PDF with executive summaries, detailed findings, and specific design recommendations.
